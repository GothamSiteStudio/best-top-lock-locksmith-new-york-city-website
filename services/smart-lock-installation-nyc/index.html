Convenience matters, but fit matters more

Smart lock installations go wrong when customers buy based only on features. In the real world, door thickness, frame clearance, buzzer systems, latch type, existing deadbolt prep, and even hallway Wi-Fi coverage can change what makes sense. That is why we start with the door and the use case, not the app marketing.

Some customers want a simple keypad so family members can stop hiding spare keys. Others want app access for deliveries, guests, cleaners, or short-term controlled entry. Some buildings have restrictions that make a discreet retrofit the better move. Our role is to help you choose a lock that works smoothly with your door, your building, and your expectations after installation.

Good reasons to install a smart lock

  • You want to stop handing out physical keys for everyday access.
  • You need temporary codes for staff, guests, or family members.
  • You are upgrading an apartment or condo for convenience without losing security.
  • You want a smart lock paired with a proper deadbolt or top lock setup.
